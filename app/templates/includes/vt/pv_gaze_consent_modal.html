{# Peripheral Vision — Gaze Validation Consent Modal Phase 1: consent + privacy notice. All processing is client-side only; no video or biometric data is sent to the server. #}

👁 Optional: Gaze Validation

This game can optionally use your front camera to verify that you keep your eyes on the central fixation cross during play. All analysis runs entirely on your device.

  • No video frames or images are sent to the server
  • No iris coordinates or face data leave your browser
  • Only a summary (e.g. "kept gaze 87% of the time") is saved

Skipping the camera is fine — your attempt will still be counted normally.
